Our first product

IduScore

IduScore is our first product, and it is currently in development.

It turns routine blood test values plus a short lifestyle questionnaire into clear, easy to understand insights on how your wellbeing may evolve over the next 10 years.

It covers four areas: cardiovascular (heart), cognitive (brain), respiratory (lungs), and renal (kidneys).
